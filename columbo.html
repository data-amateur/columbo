Just one more thing about Lt. Columbo

Columbo is a homicide detective with the Los Angeles Police Department who is known for his shambling, disheveled appearance and his persistent questioning of suspects. Despite his unkempt appearance and absent-minded manner, Columbo is a highly skilled and dedicated detective who is able to solve even the most complex of crimes. He is known for his ability to get suspects to reveal their guilt through his subtle and seemingly innocuous questioning. Columbo is also known for his catchphrase "Just one more thing..." which he often uses before asking a suspect a crucial piece of evidence that leads to their arrest. Overall, Columbo is a cunning and clever detective, with a unique approach to solving crimes.
